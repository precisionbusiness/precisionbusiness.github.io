Why Use Us

We understand your requirements, investment objectives, type of business/industry, size of business and transaction ability

Our Transaction Experience

Our expertise is backed up by over 18 years in the listing and selling of small to medium sized businesses as licensed business agents. We have insights into industries such as food & hospitality, health & beauty, import/wholesale/distribution, education, tourism, agribusiness and manufacturing. This enables us to know what are the common and acceptable parameters of gross margins, expenses on cost of goods, staff wages, rent, utilities and so on. These parameters are benchmarked to different levels of turnover, specific to each industry. Through our experience we are able to recognise if sale prices are correctly tied to the running costs and profits, in line with market expectations.

Business Search Network

Benefit From Our Experience
