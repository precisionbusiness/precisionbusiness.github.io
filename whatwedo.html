What We Do

We specialise in assisting clients with securing, negotiating & buying profitable small to medium sized businesses in Melbourne and other regions in Australia, with the further option of management support.

Business Buying Services

At Precision Business Management, we guide you on how to buy a business in Melbourne. This includes:

  • Working with you to clarify your objectives and budget
  • Sourcing suitable opportunities that meet your requirements
  • Negotiating the price and conditions with the business seller
  • Conducting due diligence, including conducting trials according to the client’s requirements.
  • Finalising legal and financial paperwork for the takeover of the business
  • Organising all paperwork and coordinating with solicitors to prepare the contract of sale, lease and stock count for the transfer of business.
  • Conducting the final settlement and takeover of the business
  • Arranging independent solicitors, accountants and consultants to assist you

Business Management & Support

We can find the right business to suit your objectives. If needed, we can also support you in the following ways:

  • Restructuring and ongoing management support
  • Management to ensure Key Performance Targets are in line with industry standards
  • Assistance with marketing and sales promotions
  • Management of account payable, including payments to suppliers and employees
  • Book keeping and quarterly BAS statement submission
  • Preparation of quarterly, half yearly and annual financial statements
  • Preparation and submission of company tax returns with a qualified accountant
  • Strategies for an exit sale

Any business sale is managed by the other business arm of ours, Precision Business Agency & Xcllusive Business Sales.
