About Us

Our aim is to search and secure the best possible outcome for you

Our team has over 18 years extensive experience as licensed agents in the listing and selling of small to medium sized businesses in food & hospitality, health & beauty, import/wholesale/distribution, education, tourism, agribusiness and manufacturing. We have helped numerous applicants through our business migration process in Australia. Our experts will help you buy a business that meets your expectation in Melbourne, as well as other regions in Australia. We have experience servicing clients of all demographics, particularly the Mandarin speaking community. At Precision Business Management, we also have dedicated Mandarin speaking staff to assist you.
